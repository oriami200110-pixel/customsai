2. Non-Binding Nature of AI Outputs

Classification results: (a) are not binding on any customs authority; (b) do not constitute a CBP binding ruling under 19 CFR Part 177; (c) should be independently verified by a licensed customs broker before formal entry filing.

Tariffly’s total aggregate liability shall not exceed the greater of $100 USD or fees paid in the prior 30 days. Tariffly shall not be liable for customs penalties, back-duties, or seizure of goods.
