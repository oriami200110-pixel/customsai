1. Nature of Service

CustomsAI provides automated HTS tariff classification suggestions using AI. All outputs are informational only and do not constitute legal advice, binding customs rulings, or professional customs brokerage services under 19 U.S.C. § 1641.

2. Non-Binding Nature of AI Outputs

Results: (a) are not binding on any customs authority; (b) do not constitute a CBP binding ruling under 19 CFR Part 177; (c) should be independently verified by a licensed customs broker before formal entry filing.

3. Limitation of Liability

CustomsAI's total aggregate liability shall not exceed the greater of $100 USD or fees paid in the prior 30 days. CustomsAI shall not be liable for customs penalties, back-duties, or seizure of goods.

4. User Responsibilities
